WELCOME TO MY HOBBIES
GAMING

Video games improve co-ordination, attention, concentration and problem-solving skills. It also enhances memory, helps in multitasking capability and can also be used as excellent teaching tools.

COOKING

Cooking skills may help people to meet nutrition guidelines in their daily nutrition supply.They allow people to eat good food.A food is something that provides nutrients. Nutrients are substances that provide: energy for activity, growth, and all functions of the body such as breathing, digesting food, and keeping warm; materials for the growth and repair of the body, and for keeping the immune system healthy.

BASKETBALL

Basketball is a team sport in which two teams, most commonly of five players each, opposing one another on a rectangular court, compete . This sport is worth playing . It gives complete exercise to you.Basketball is one of the most popular sports in the world and for good reason.It is one of the best sports for both physical and emotional health and benefits.


GYMING

It is a known fact that regular exercise benefits your health, mind, and body. It boosts your energy, increases lean muscle mass, decreases your risk for certain health conditions, and helps you manage your weight. Moreover, it also improves your mood and enables you to live longer.The good news is you have a lot of options when it comes to exercise.

TRAVELLING

Benefit of travel is the experience you gain. Every trip you undertake adds to your life experience. This is because when you travel, you experience things that you could never encounter at home. You visit beautiful places and landscapes, meet fellow travelers and locals, indulge yourself in the local culture, etc. Even though these all sound glamorous, there are also times that you have to cope with unfamiliar situations, problems, and challenges. The skills and experience you gain from these moments give you life-long personal benefits as well as an advantage in the professional world. Overall, it makes you more interesting as a person.
